🧠 Skill Balance Challenge

You are participating in multiple coding contests.
Each contest is defined by two values:

SkillPoints → Points gained if you lose the contest

Priority

1 → Important contest

0 → Unimportant contest

Rules

You may lose at most k important contests.

Losing a contest adds SkillPoints.

Winning a contest subtracts SkillPoints.

You may lose all unimportant contests freely.

🎯 Objective:
Maximize your total Skill Balance.

🔹 Function Signature
public static int skillBalance(int k, List<List<int>> contests)

Parameters

k → Maximum number of important contests you are allowed to lose

contests[i] → A list of two integers:

contests[i][0] = SkillPoints

contests[i][1] = Priority (0 or 1)

Returns

An integer representing the maximum possible Skill Balance

🔹 Sample Input
int k = 2;

List<List<int>> contests = new List<List<int>>()
{
    new List<int>{10, 1},
    new List<int>{5, 1},
    new List<int>{8, 1},
    new List<int>{7, 0},
    new List<int>{4, 0}
};

🔹 Explanation
Step 1: Separate contests by priority
SkillPoints	Priority	Contest Type
10	1	Important
5	1	Important
8	1	Important
7	0	Unimportant
4	0	Unimportant
Step 2: Handle unimportant contests

You can lose all unimportant contests:

+7 + 4 = +11

Step 3: Handle important contests

You may lose at most k = 2 important contests.

Sort important contests by SkillPoints in descending order:

10, 8, 5


Lose the top k contests:

+10 + 8 = +18


Win the remaining contest:

-5

Step 4: Calculate final Skill Balance
Category	Points
Unimportant contests	+11
Important contests	+18 − 5 = +13
✅ Final Skill Balance
11 + 13 = 24

🧪 Expected Output
24
